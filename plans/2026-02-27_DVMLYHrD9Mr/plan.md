# Implement Psychological Trigger Copy Across All Customer Touchpoints

**Source:** [Max Sturtevant](https://www.instagram.com/reels/DVMLYHrD9Mr/)
**Category:** marketing
**Relevance:** 90%
**Total Hours:** 17.0h
**Status:** review

## Summary

Replace discount-focused messaging with four high-converting psychological triggers: identity-based copy, 'on us' reframing, bandwagon positioning, and specific data points. This will increase conversion rates by leveraging consistency bias and social proof instead of competing on price.

## Key Insights

- We could rewrite our service descriptions using identity-based copy like 'You want your business to run while you sleep, automate it like it' to trigger consistency bias in local service owners
- Apply this by changing all our 'free' language to 'on us' - 'Your website setup is on us', 'First month of automation is on us', 'Ad strategy session is on us'
- We could position our AI automation as mainstream/growing instead of cutting-edge by saying 'AI automation is the fastest-growing business solution for local contractors in 2024'
- Apply this by gathering specific client data and using numbers like '87% of our clients book 40% more appointments in their first 60 days' instead of generic 'our clients love us'
- We could create email sequences around bandwagon positioning like 'Why every HVAC company is switching to AI appointment booking' to create FOMO
- Apply this by surveying current clients for specific metrics we can promote - response times, booking increases, time saved per week

## Tasks

### 1. Rewrite website homepage and service pages with identity-based copy
**Priority:** high | **Hours:** 3.0h | **Tools:** website, claude_code

Replace existing headlines and service descriptions with identity-based copy that triggers consistency bias. Homepage hero: 'Your business should run while you sleep, automate it like it' replacing current headline. Services page: 'You want more appointments without more phone calls - let AI handle the bookings while you focus on the work' for chatbot service. 'You want your website working 24/7 to bring in leads - set it up once, profit forever' for website service. Update all 'free' language to 'on us': 'Your website setup is on us', 'Your first month of automation is on us', 'Strategy session is on us' throughout all pages.

**Deliverables:**
- Updated homepage copy with identity-based headlines
- Rewritten service page descriptions using consistency bias
- All 'free' language converted to 'on us' across website

### 2. Survey current clients for specific performance metrics
**Priority:** high | **Hours:** 2.0h | **Tools:** ghl

Create and send a GHL survey to all active clients asking: 1) Appointment booking increase percentage since implementing AI, 2) Days to first AI-booked appointment, 3) Hours saved per week on admin tasks, 4) Response time improvement. Target getting responses from at least 10 clients to calculate percentages for social proof copy. Follow up via phone calls if needed to hit minimum response rate.

**Deliverables:**
- GHL survey form with 4 specific performance questions
- Collected data from minimum 10 clients
- Calculated statistics for copy use (booking increases, time to results, time saved)

### 3. Create bandwagon positioning email sequence
**Priority:** high | **Hours:** 4.0h | **Tools:** ghl, claude_code

Write a 5-email sequence in GHL using bandwagon psychology instead of elitist positioning. Email 1 subject: 'Why every HVAC company is switching to AI appointment booking' - body focuses on industry trend, not exclusivity. Email 2: 'AI automation is the fastest-growing business solution for local contractors in 2024' with mainstream adoption stats. Email 3: 'You feel left behind while competitors book 40% more jobs' - FOMO-driven. Email 4: 'Why this local plumber is breaking the internet' - case study format. Email 5: 'Your first month of automation is on us (but only this week)' - close with 'on us' language.

**Deliverables:**
- 5-email sequence written in GHL with bandwagon psychology
- Subject lines using swipe phrases
- Email bodies focusing on mainstream adoption vs. exclusivity

### 4. Update Meta ads with psychological triggers and 'on us' language
**Priority:** medium | **Hours:** 2.5h | **Tools:** meta_ads, claude_code

Rewrite all active Meta ad campaigns using the four psychological triggers. Primary ads: 'Your business should run while you sleep, automate it like it' as headline. 'Your website setup is on us' and 'Strategy session is on us' as call-to-action copy. Replace any generic claims with specific data once collected from client survey. Create new ad variations with bandwagon positioning: 'AI automation is the fastest-growing business solution for local contractors in 2024' and specific metrics like '87% of our clients book 40% more appointments in their first 60 days'.

**Deliverables:**
- Updated ad headlines using identity-based copy
- All CTAs converted from 'free' to 'on us' language
- New ad variations with bandwagon positioning and specific metrics

**Depends on:** Survey current clients for specific performance metrics

### 5. Build automated follow-up sequence with specific data points
**Priority:** medium | **Hours:** 3.5h | **Tools:** n8n, ghl, claude_code

Create an n8n workflow that triggers when someone books a strategy call. The sequence sends a series of messages with specific performance data instead of generic testimonials. Message 1 (immediate): 'Your strategy session is on us - here's what to expect when 87% of our clients book 40% more appointments'. Message 2 (day after): '93% of our contractors get their first AI-booked appointment within 14 days'. Message 3 (3 days): 'Your business should run while you sleep, automate it like it - let's make it happen'. Include SMS and email versions in GHL.

**Deliverables:**
- n8n workflow triggering on calendar bookings
- 3-message follow-up sequence with specific metrics
- Both SMS and email versions in GHL

**Depends on:** Survey current clients for specific performance metrics

### 6. Update all outreach templates with psychological triggers
**Priority:** low | **Hours:** 2.0h | **Tools:** ghl, claude_code

Rewrite cold email templates, LinkedIn messages, and call scripts using identity-based copy and 'on us' language. Cold email: Start with 'Your business should run while you sleep, automate it like it' and end with 'Your strategy session is on us - when works better, Tuesday or Thursday?'. LinkedIn: 'AI automation is the fastest-growing business solution for local contractors in 2024 - your setup is on us'. Call script: Focus on bandwagon positioning 'Most contractors in [area] are switching to AI booking' rather than 'exclusive opportunity'.

**Deliverables:**
- Updated cold email templates with identity-based copy
- LinkedIn outreach messages using 'on us' language
- Call scripts with bandwagon positioning
