## Summary

Build a Claude connector integrated with n8n workflows to generate verified local contractor leads through conversational AI. Position our service as turning websites into prospecting workspaces while capturing social media engagement through comment triggers.

## Key Insights

- We could create our own Claude connector for local service lead generation - integrate with our existing n8n workflows to pull HVAC, plumber, roofing contractor data directly through Claude conversations
- Apply the 'comment to get access' strategy on our social posts - use 'Comment LEADS' or 'Comment SETUP' to capture engagement and funnel prospects into our automated follow-up sequences
- We could position our AI chatbot service as turning their website 'into a full prospecting workspace' - reframe from just lead capture to active lead generation system
- Apply the instant structured output approach - show prospects exactly what they'll get (emails, phone numbers, company data) rather than vague promises about 'more leads'
- We could create a local service version: 'Just type Find me 50 Dallas HVAC contractors and it returns structured lists instantly' - adapt for our target market
- Use the 'verified contact info' angle heavily - emphasize our lead quality and verification process to differentiate from cheap lead lists

## Tasks

### 1. Build Claude Lead Generation Connector
**Priority:** high | **Hours:** 6.0h | **Tools:** n8n, claude_code

Create n8n workflow that connects to Claude API and local business databases. When triggered, Claude processes requests like 'Find me 50 Dallas HVAC contractors' and returns structured data with company names, emails, phone numbers, and addresses. Integrate with local business APIs and Yellow Pages data sources.

**Deliverables:**
- n8n workflow with Claude API integration
- Structured JSON output format for lead data
- Connection to 3+ local business data sources
